Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do You Sell Domain Names?

    We do not offer domain registration. But there are a number of great registrars out there. If you have questions about registering domains, feel free to get in touch - we’d be happy to help!

  • Do You Offer Email Accounts?

    No. But we highly recommend that our customers work with a 3rd party email provider that specializes in email hosting. Our top recommendations are G Suite and Rackspace Email.

  • How Long Does it Take to Setup My Account?

    All accounts are activated automatically upon payment. If this is your first order with us, our team may contact you with the request for verification details.

  • Can I Upgrade/Downgrade My Account?

    Yes, at any time through our client area.

  • Will you help me migrate my WordPress site?

    Absolutely. Simply submit a migration request ticket through our control panel and we’ll get your site transferred.

  • Where Are Your Servers Located?

    We offer 8 server locations worldwide.
